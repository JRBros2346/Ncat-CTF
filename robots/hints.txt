CTF Challenge: "fsociety's Backdoor Hunt"

Background Story:
You’ve infiltrated Evil Corp’s network, but you know they’ve set up a backdoor to monitor you. The challenge is to identify the backdoor server, hidden among 100 potential IP addresses. You have 5 attempts to guess the correct one, and after 3 wrong guesses, you will receive a hint.

Objective:
Guess the correct IP address where the backdoor server is located. The hint system will assist you as you close in on the solution.
